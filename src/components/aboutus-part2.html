About

OguName.com

OguName is a web-based project to archive personal names within the ogu culture. As captured in the logo. 'name' is 'Yinko' (or'oyin') in the gun language of ogu people of the west Africa coast. The project documents the meanings of and the roots of the names in English language


OguName.com is part of broader initiative to promote digital inclusion and the preservation of gun language. it is a contribution towards the documentation of various aspects of the people's culture expression, identities, and history on the worldwide web


Inspired by YorubaName.com project, and the pioneering work of the late ogu local historian, chief Theophilus Ola Avoseh, OguName.com is a crowdsourced platform run by volunteer culture and language enthusiasts.


We count on donations and supports towards sustaining this initiative
